What is the Resilient Smart Garden? A way to care for your plants efficiently

The Resilient Smart Garden is a garden system that minimizes water usage while maintaining the habitat of the plants. The health of the garden will be monitored by a temperature sensor, humidity sensor, and moisture sensor to ensure your garden is growing under ideal conditions. The moisture sensor will keep track of the soil's moisture and will ensure plants recieve the appropriate amount of water when it is needed. A threshold limit is put in place for each sensor to automatically water the plant.

Monitor your garden from Anywhere Mobile and web app let you check on your garden from anywhere!

The resilient Smart Garden comes with accompanying apps that will make checking in on your garden and tracking it's growth easy. Web, Android, and iOS apps are available
